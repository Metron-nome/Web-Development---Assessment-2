Story

She awoke from her dream.

The wind howled fiercely at the rooftop’s edge as she stretched, squinting down at the lively streets below. Vehicles crisscrossed the roads, their taillights forming a vibrant, fiery chain. Resting her chin on her hand, she began to hum a tune she learned from Rover — secretly.


They were not enemies, yet far from allies. Rover caught the attention of factions, including her own. But Camellya didn’t care. She was a “chosen seed,” made for one purpose — to hunt Rover. Injections dulled her mind, clouded her senses, but her longing for Rover stayed sharp.


Vines crept around her arms as she perched on a rooftop edge, laughing softly at her fantasy. “I’ll give them a flower,” she thought. A blossom from her very being — one that wouldn’t wilt unless she did. Then the thought was gone, swallowed by chaos.


“Little did she know, centuries later, the scene before her would vanish in the Lament.”


She would wander through memory’s labyrinth, meet Rover by a lake in Petalfall Village, and join the Black Shores. They would share hundreds of years, separate, and reunite in Jinzhou — a cycle of partings and fate-driven blooms.


She leapt. Vines trailed through the city, seeking them.

This is Camellya’s story — not of purpose, but of longing, memory, and a flower that never fades.

Overview

Camellya is a 5-star Havoc character wielding a Sword. She excels in dealing Havoc damage through her unique Blossom Mode and versatile skill set.

Skills

  • Basic Attack: 5-strike combo dealing Havoc DMG. Stage 4+ enables Hold Attack continuation.
  • Heavy Attack – Pruning: Consumes stamina to deal burst Havoc DMG.
  • Resonance Skill – Crimson Blossom: Inflicts Havoc DMG and activates Blossom Mode.
  • Resonance Liberation: Mid-air burst dealing high Havoc DMG.
  • Forte Circuit: Converts Crimson Pistils into Crimson Buds, boosting damage and regen.
